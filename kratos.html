BLT Sandwich

Kratos, The God Of War.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of Unbridled Fury
  • 2 tablespoons of Betrayal
  • A pinch of Godly Ambition
  • 3 cups of Guilt, finely chopped
  • A dash of Vengeance
  • 1 cup of Wisdom, aged
  • A heaping spoonful of Paternal Love
  • A sprinkle of Hope
  • A handful of Regret
  • A generous serving of Inner Demons

Instructions:

  1. Marinate in Betrayal: Start by marinating the protagonist, Kratos, in a heavy sauce of Betrayal. This is the dark secret that sets the story in motion.
  2. Simmer in Unbridled Fury: Add the Unbridled Fury to the pot and let it simmer. Kratos's rage against the gods should be the driving force of the narrative.
  3. Stir in Godly Ambition: Mix in a pinch of Godly Ambition to represent Kratos's ascent to godhood and his thirst for power.
  4. Sprinkle with Vengeance: As the story unfolds, sprinkle in some Vengeance, emphasizing Kratos's relentless pursuit of justice against those who wronged him.
  5. Balance with Guilt: Gradually add the finely chopped Guilt to balance the recipe. This represents the emotional weight Kratos carries for his past actions.
  6. Season with Wisdom: Incorporate the aged Wisdom to symbolize Kratos's transformation and growing understanding of the consequences of his actions.
  7. Stir in Paternal Love: As the story progresses, stir in the heaping spoonful of Paternal Love. This represents Kratos's newfound role as a father and mentor to his son, Atreus.
  8. Sprinkle with Hope: To add depth and complexity, sprinkle a bit of Hope to signify Kratos's journey towards redemption.
  9. Garnish with Regret: Don't forget to garnish the stew with a handful of Regret, underscoring the character's inner turmoil.
  10. Serve with Inner Demons: Finally, serve the Redemption Stew with a generous serving of Inner Demons. These are the personal struggles and conflicts that Kratos must confront throughout his journey.

In this analogy, Kratos's journey is likened to the creation of a complex and flavorful stew. It's a recipe for redemption that teaches us that even the most bitter of ingredients can be transformed into something meaningful and nourishing.
